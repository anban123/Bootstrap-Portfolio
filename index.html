About Me

I am a junior developer leveraging a background in business and real estate to try and build a more comprehensive user experience on the web. I have a certificate in full stack web development from the University of Denver (2019) with newly developed skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Nodejs, mySQL, Sequelize, MongoDB, Mongoose, Github, Heroku, and React.js. I also have experience in retail, real estate, and marketing and have worked on a number of freelance web development projects. With each project, my aim is to create a stylish and effortless user experience. I look forward to continuing my career in a fast paced, quality driven team environment to make the web a better experience for everyone.
